Calligraphy

Calligraphy is an art long-gone hidden behind other forms of arts. I was never -- and still am -- good at being artistic, but I discovered a love for Arabic calligraphy back in high school. I took a class in Arabic calligraphy with an expert for a teacher, and it was absolutely one of my best experiences. I ended up doing my senior project on Calligraphy, in both English and Arabic. Since then, calligraphy became something of a rare hobby but forever my love. Please, enjoy few of my attempts at Arabic calligraphy in the gallery below.

Writing out names calligraphically soothes my heart, purifies my soul, breathes cleanliness into my blood, and ties me closer to the Lord, the One who beautifies the language of Arabic.
